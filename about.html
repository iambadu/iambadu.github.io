Me

Hi there, my name is Emmanuel Badu Sarpong, I am a designer and frontend developer. Currently I am the Lead Designer and Creative Director at Codecliq. I have been fascinated by computers since the first day I saw one. Along the way, design found me, and I have been in love with good design ever since. I am a self-starter and I prefer self-education to other forms of education.

Design

Ever since I could remember I have been attracted to good design. The design is not only about form but it is also about function. It is not only about how it looks but also how it works. I am very fascinated by the how and structure of things. This fascination drew me to design and over the years, I have learned as much as I can about all that a good design entails. Over the years, I have studied Typography, Layout and Composition, Principles of Design, Color Theory, Photoshop, Indesign and Illustrator
