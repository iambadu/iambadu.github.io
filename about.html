Me

Hi there, my name is Emmanuel Badu Sarpong, a Ghanaian self taught designer and frontend developer, who is currently finding his way in Europe. I have been fascinated by computers since the first day i saw one. Along the way, design found me, and I have been in love with good design ever since. I am a self starter and i prefer self education to other forms of education. I have spent the last 5yrs of my life learning about web design, frontend development, graphic design and I am currently learning ux design and backend development.

Ever since i could remember I have been attracted to good design. Design is not oly about form but it is also about function. It is not only about how it looks but also how it works.
